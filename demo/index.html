Press an arrow key to start the game!

  1. The Rules

    1. Eat the baits by running into them, each bait eaten increase your snake size.

    2. The bigger your snake is, the faster he goes.

    3. Reaching one screen edge will teleport you to the other side of the screen.

    4. If you eat your own tail: GAME OVER.

  2. Controls

    Use your keyboard's arrow keys to move your snake in any direction.
